Backbase challenge app help

When the app starts up for the first time, an empty list of bookmarked places is shown. To add new bookmarks of places, you can tap the map icon at the top of the navigation bar to go the map.

You can hold press on a desired position over the map to add a new pin.

When you finish placing the locations, you can go back to the previous screen to see the list of the places you selected previously. To delete a bookmarked place just swipe left to show the delete button.

You can select any of the rows in the table to show the selected city forecast.
